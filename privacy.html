1. Who we are

NursingEdge ("NursingEdge," "we," "us") provides a nursing excellence and governance platform for healthcare organizations. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and our platform, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact and demo-request information you submit through our forms (name, organization, title, email, phone, and any message you provide).
  • Platform account and usage data for customers using NursingEdge, including governance, quality, and clinical ladder records entered by your organization's users.
  • Basic technical data (such as browser type, device, and pages visited) collected automatically when you use this site.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to demo requests and inquiries.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the NursingEdge platform for our customers.
  • To communicate with you about your account or our services.
  • To meet legal, security, and compliance obligations.

4. Healthcare data

Data entered into the NursingEdge platform by customer organizations (such as governance minutes, quality projects, and clinical ladder records) is handled under the terms of the applicable customer agreement between NursingEdge and the healthcare organization. Where required, a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) or equivalent data protection terms will govern the handling of any protected health information.

5. How we protect information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information, including encryption of data in transit, role-based access controls, and tenant data isolation. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate this site and our platform (for example, hosting and form-processing providers), or as required by law.
