How it works

From daily activity to accreditation evidence

NursingEdge builds the evidence chain automatically, so nothing needs to be reconstructed at survey time.

Capture

Councils meet, projects run, nurses advance. Every activity is recorded where it happens — no extra documentation burden.

Connect

AI links activities, minutes, and outcomes to each other and to the people and units involved.

Map

Evidence is mapped to the right ANCC Magnet standard and section — automatically, with your team in control.

Prove

Stories, exports, and dashboards deliver survey-ready evidence with a full, auditable trail behind every claim.

Why NursingEdge exists

Built by someone who lived the problem

After 17+ years in nursing quality, patient safety, and accreditation — across leading institutions and as a national accreditation surveyor — one frustration kept repeating: the evidence of nursing excellence existed everywhere, but never in one place.

So he learned to code and built the platform he always needed: one hub where governance, quality, and professional development connect — and where Magnet readiness is a byproduct of daily work, not a last-minute scramble.
